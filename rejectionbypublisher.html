OBITUARY *
 
Eleanor E. Hanley (nee Keller), 95, Of Tenafly NJ passed on May 1, 2025 with her guardian at her side. Born 1929, Daughter of Walter & Edna (O'Connor). Walter was a Chauffer at the time & later operated Joe Judge's (Windmill Bar/Grill/Restaurant) on Rte 59/Nanuet Turnpike NY. Wife of Francis(d. 2008), a carpenter employed by Massa & JD Construction. Later union member projects: Bergen Record bldg, Meadowlands Stadium, Haworth water plant, Rockland hospital, Route 287 extension. Francis was pre-deceased by siblings: Joseph J. Sr of Englewood, Ruth Jordan of New Milford, many others unknown.

Born in the Bronx. Attended Our Savior Catholic Church & School. Her family moved to Ft Lee NJ after the GW Bridge opened. Attended Madonna School. Graduated 1947, St. Cecilia High School Englewood NJ. Her graduating classmates: Francis brother: Arthur Hanley & her classmates' sister Lena married her brother-in-law Joseph J Hanley Sr.

As a young girl she worked all over Ft Lee (Schweitzers/Gerbers/Marchands). Holy Name Hospital with her cousin Rosemarie Kucera (nee Hawkins). In her twenties at NY Life Insurance (Madison Square Park, NYC) policy briefs department as a secretary.

Her future husband Francis, a veteran of the US Navy (WWII. Seaman 1st class: DD-796) was a friend of her sister's boyfriend Scotty. Eleanor was married at Madonna Church of Ft Lee(1954) and several years later started raising children. 1962, the family moved to Englewood. Was a parishioner of St. Cecilia Church. 1972, Moved to Tenafly NJ. Then parishioner of Mt. Carmel, Tenafly NJ. The occasional daily am mass and some high holy days at SMA Fathers (African Mission). The last several years: St. Johns, Sat:5:30pm & St Cecilia, Sun:6pm. Prior to hospital admission, 4/26. Mom was still asking, "What time is mass"?

Predeceased by her Sister Kathleen (Class of 1948) who was married to Francis friend William Dickie(aka Scotty. D.1971) owner of the Touchdown Club, Fort Lee NJ. Her brother James Stephen (D. 2023). Surviving: Walter Francis. Sons: Richard & David.

Thank you mom for everything. Taking us ice skating as children & the beach. The food, Oh my! Hungarian goulash, Rosti, shrimp Scampi, beef Stew, homemade Swedish meatballs, chicken Cacciatore. Pancakes & French Toast. We love you! Your sons. Miss your presence already. I can't believe your not here.

Eleanor was a resident of Tenafly NJ for 52+ years. She worked at Tenafly High School cafeteria for a period. Her husband while working on the construction of Paramus Park Mall told her of a new store hiring sales staff. Now an employee at Abraham & Straus (A&S) which became Macy's. She participated in organizing the bus trips to NYC for performances at Lincoln Center & Broadway theatre. Working at the store for 30+ years.

A member of the Tenafly Woman's Club, Borough of Tenafly Welcome Committee and worked the Tenafly NJ elections. She was a book reader and frequent visitor to the Tenafly NJ library and Tenafly NJ Swim Club where she would take books out.

2015, trespassing by the Borough of Tenafly began. 2017 family member interrogated by Code Enforcement officer. A repair permit was ordered when a repair was not being done. The roof replacement ended up being faulty. At next visit/interrogation when a question wasn't answered a summons was issued for a pile of twigs and a piece of wood. Several months later a trial was initiated by prosecutor. At Tenafly NJ municipal court a family member was put on trial instead & not allowed to speak due to prosecutor objection. $283 fine paid. Post pandemic her family was blocked from taking out books for her at the Borough of Tenafly NJ library. Later a new enforcement officer for property maintenance began trespassing and filing multiple lawsuits against the residents of Tenafly. Code officer then pounded on the front door causing damage. Code officer did engage in organizing a raid and removal with another borough official. Pounding on front door by Tenafly police all with their hands on firearm holsters. Personal effects were removed & destroyed. Yard was destroyed by a tractor used by a private contractor. Another fine. Code Officer then began to stalk the home on regular basis parking outside staring into the home. In the months following waving to the family inside the home. Waving summons to the family inside home. Taking photos of the family inside the home.

Months later Borough of Tenafly Police & borough administrator came to cut the lawn now overgrown by another contractor. Another fine. Months later Code officer did begin to work with former borough official. Resident was then assaulted by former borough official who was reported to police. A lien was threatened a year later. $6052.91 in fines paid. Guardian went to pay property tax which was not accepted resulting in another fiasco. The property maintenance code officer was then removed from borough of Tenafly NJ employ. For several years residents did experience injuries, traumatization, nervous disorder, psychological disorder, ptsd, damage to home and property. Tree causing damage to home unable to be removed, cancellation of newspaper delivery & an illegal entry to the home. On 4/24 former borough member did assault & threaten her guardian again. 911 was called. During which Eleanor was deprived of care for hours and a special procedure had to be performed & again the following day. 4/26 Admitted to the Hospital and left us with her spirit on 5/1. Support the cause: EleanorOfTenaflyNJ.com.

Thanks to all nurses and doctors who may have tended to mother during her life. Funeral mass for the lady: 5/17 (Sat), 10:30am. Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church, 10 County Rd, Tenafly, NJ.

 * Note: The above obituary was not fully published by The Bergen Record newspaper or online at Northjersey.com website. Instead the paragraphs regarding the abuses by the Borough Of Tenafly were removed. This may be in violation of the rules of free speech.
